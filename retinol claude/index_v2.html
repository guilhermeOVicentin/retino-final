Fine lines are not flaws — they are the natural signature of a life lived. Yet with the quiet, consistent work of retinal, your skin holds the remarkable ability to rewrite itself.

Why fine lines appear

As we age, the skin's natural production of collagen and elastin gradually slows. Combined with daily sun exposure, environmental aggressors, and the subtle repetition of facial expressions, the skin loses its capacity to spring back as it once did. What begins as barely perceptible creases at the corners of the eyes or across the forehead deepens, quietly, over time.

Retinal — the more potent, faster-acting precursor to retinoic acid — intervenes at a cellular level. Applied consistently each evening, it accelerates skin-cell renewal, encourages collagen synthesis, and gently resurfaces the upper layers of skin, progressively revealing a smoother, more refined complexion beneath.

Visibly softened fine lines With consistent nightly use, expression lines around the eyes and forehead begin to appear progressively smoother and less defined over 6–8 weeks.
A fresher, more luminous surface As retinal encourages cellular turnover, dullness lifts and skin takes on a refined radiance — the kind that appears rested and quietly renewed.
Long-term skin resilience Beyond the surface, retinal supports the skin's structural integrity — improving elasticity and firmness with sustained use, for complexion that feels genuinely strengthened from within.

Individual results vary. Visible improvement is typically experienced with consistent use over 6–12 weeks. As with all active ingredients, some initial adjustment may occur. We recommend introducing retinal gradually and always wearing SPF during the day.
